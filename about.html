Microversity
Folklore Fiesta 2022

The Microversity Spring Fest, held every year, is a place where Students from all over the India and experts, scholars and famous artists who support new talents and skills contribute to this event together.

1960s - 1970s
The First Edition of Spring Fest was held with the motto "True Spirit of Youth" making it the oldest social and cultural fest around the globe
1970s - 2010s
Commenced as a platform for the Micronauts to showcase their skills, extended its boundaries and gained traction in Maharashtra
2010s - 2020s
Fest grew in scale, attracting participants from different parts of India and across the world
2021
Continuing the legacy of Predecessors, Spring Fest became Asia's largest and oldest social and cultural fest even in the tough times and became huge success too.
2022
Join us to Unlock the mystery of old times. Explore the Folklore Fiesta
